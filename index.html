Moonshine Exchange

The world's most efficient cross chain exchange

Moonshine was founded on the idea that digital assets should be accessible to all at a negligable cost.

Safe

Moonshine users hold the keys to their crypto which means it can't be confiscated by miners or any third party.

Stable

The currency used in the Moonshine network is USDC. USDC is pegged to the dollar and fully backed by US dollars.

Open

Instead of being closed to tokens on a single blockchain Moonshine will be open to all crypto networks that support USDC.
